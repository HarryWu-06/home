One of the most popular and best sandbox games in the world, I started playing the game when I was 7 because of the huge hype. But I only have the Chinese version which is not as good as the international one(fewer input and ways to play) D: SAD. I really wish to have a international version.



[PubG]

PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds (PUBG) is an online multiplayer battle royale game, this game was very popular in 2016, and the mobile version came out in 2018. Me and my classmates played it a lot, I have lots of good memories of this game. But now they all have no time to play(studying) and left me alone…(it's a sad story, isn't it?)
