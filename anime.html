Anime

Anime is a style of animation originating in Japan that is characterized by stark colorful graphics depicting vibrant characters in action-filled plots often with fantastic or futuristic themes.
Either the long and passionate Dragon Ball, One Piece, Naruto, Pokemon...(first picture) or the masterpiece like My Neighbor Totoro, Spirited Away...(second picture) by Hayao Miyazaki are all regarded as Animes.
